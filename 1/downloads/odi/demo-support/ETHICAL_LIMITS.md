# Ethical Limits

VEHICLE-ODI is designed as an auditable research and decision-support architecture for space safety.

It does not authorize debris removal operations by itself.

It must not be used for offensive space capabilities, concealment of harmful operations, unauthorized targeting of spacecraft or automated destructive decisions.

Proper use is scientific, educational, defensive, legal-technical, policy-oriented and human-supervised.

Removal operations require consent, ownership review, registry review, liability allocation, safety validation and legal authorization.
