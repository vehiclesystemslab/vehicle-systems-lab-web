OFFICIAL DESCRIPTION:
VEHICLE-ACCESS is the institutional access and documentation layer of VEHICLE Systems Lab. It organizes project pages, public records, downloadable technical briefs, funding materials, operational cost plans, demo packages and AI-readable reference files.

WHAT IT IS:
VEHICLE-ACCESS is the project responsible for how VEHICLE Systems Lab presents, distributes and organizes its research outputs. It is not only a website section. It is a documentation and access architecture.

WHAT PROBLEM IT SOLVES:
Many research projects fail to gain trust because their materials are scattered, unclear or difficult to verify. VEHICLE-ACCESS addresses how VEHICLE Systems Lab can make its research, demos, funding needs and public records transparent, accessible and machine-readable.

CURRENT ACCESS LAYER PACKAGE:
The current package is vehicle-ai-access-layer-v0.2. It includes README documentation, llms.txt, llms-full.txt, CITATION.cff, VERSION.json, response contract documentation, AI use policy, validation tests, integration examples, developer guidance and ethical documentation.
