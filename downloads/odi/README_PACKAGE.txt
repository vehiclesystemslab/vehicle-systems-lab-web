Purpose:
This package presents VEHICLE-ODI as an auditable orbital debris intelligence project for investors, researchers, space-sector partners, policy reviewers and technical evaluators.

- Editable source documents.

- Investor-ready PDF documents.

TXT/

DEMO/

RESPONSIBILITY_PAPER/

Ethical note:
VEHICLE-ODI is a research and decision-support architecture for space safety. It is not a weapon system and does not authorize debris removal without consent, legal review, liability allocation and safety validation.
