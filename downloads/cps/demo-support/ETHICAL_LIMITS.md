# Ethical Limits

VEHICLE-CPS is designed as an auditable research and decision-support architecture.

It does not replace human judgment, legal authority or institutional responsibility.

It must not be used for unlawful surveillance, automated coercion, political persecution, offensive targeting, repression or harmful autonomous decision-making.

Proper use is preventive, defensive, scientific, educational, institutional and human-supervised.

This demo is a limited research and exhibition artifact. It is not a deployed operational security system.
