Core architecture
01
Mother Cube

The three-dimensional structural foundation — cubic cells with directional compatibility interfaces and pyramidal compression toward a central coherent node.

02
Knowledge Layers

Information organized into finite structural layers. Each layer can be grouped, compared, reviewed, and connected across domains without losing coherence.

03
Nodes & Attractors

Nodes are fundamental structural units — objects, actors, decisions, risks, events. Attractors are structural centers that organize movement, attention, and decision pressure.

04
Contractors

Stabilizing, compressing, and transforming mechanisms that keep complexity interpretable. They prevent structural drift and preserve the integrity of each layer.

05
E.I.A.R.(V)

A VEHICLE-specific process layer for Evaluation, Interpretation, Analysis, Reasoning, and Validation — the cognitive cycle that governs how MADRE processes any domain.

06
Informational Mitosis

The process through which a new project separates from the mother structure — inheriting principles, language, and architecture while developing its own domain-specific components.
