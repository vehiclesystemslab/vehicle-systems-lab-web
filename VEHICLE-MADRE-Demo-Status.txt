VEHICLE-MADRE Demo Status

Status: In training and consolidation.

VEHICLE-MADRE does not include a public functional demo in this package yet.

Reason:
MADRE is the living structural core of VEHICLE Systems Lab. It organizes project inheritance, layers, nodes, attractors, contractors and informational mitosis. Because MADRE is the source architecture for the laboratory, its demo should be produced only after the training structure, glossary, diagrams and inheritance model are fully consolidated.

Planned future demo contents:
- Mother Cube visualization
- 2D knowledge layer demonstration
- Node and attractor mapping
- Contractor behavior example
- Informational mitosis simulation
- Project inheritance demonstration from MADRE to CPS, ODI, SUPRA and ACCESS
- Audit trail example
